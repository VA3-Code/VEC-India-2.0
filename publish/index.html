About Us

VISHAL ENGINEERING CORPORATION is an ISO 9001 Certified, precision machining unit for manufacture & exporters of Generators, Diesel Engines Parts, Sleeves, Cylider Block, Pistons & Rings components for automobiles and other engineering industries.

We introduce ourselves as manufacturer of pistons and piston rings of all kind from 35mm diameter to 130mm diameter. We are manufacturing Pistons and piston rings from last 30 years.

We have our own casting units equipped with latest machines and we manufacture components as per O. E. M .s specifications and standards. The components manufactured by us find application in all type of Two, three wheelers and motor cycle engines and also in all kind of diesel and petrol operated engines & Compressors (Air Brake compressors also).

Note : We are manufacturing piston assemblies for Chainsaws, Grass Cutters, ATVs, Lawnmowers, Brush Cutters, Sprayers, Pumps, Generators, Briggs & Stratton & Tecumseh ,Air brake compressors also.

We can also manufacture any kind of pistons and piston rings from 35mm to 130mm diameter According to the needs of our customers may it be for any kind of application.

VISHAL ENGINEERING CORPORATION has long term relationship with its customers and believes in providing quality products & services to ensure that clients are duly satisfied.
